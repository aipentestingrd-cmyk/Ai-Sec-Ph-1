Code of Conduct

The short version: treat this like a professional community, not a comment section.

  • Be respectful. Disagree on ideas, not people.
  • No harassment, discrimination, or hate speech — online or in person.
  • Boardroom discussions are Chatham House rule: share the insight, not who said it.
  • Don't use community events for unsolicited sales pitches or recruiting spam.
  • Report violations to any organizer — every complaint is taken seriously and handled discreetly.
